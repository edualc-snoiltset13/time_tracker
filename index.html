Time Tracker

A Flutter app for tracking tasks, time, and invoicing your work.

Features

Tasks & Todos

Plan your work with prioritised tasks, deadlines, and project tagging. Search across title, description, category, and project.

Time Tracking

Start a timer from any task and stop it when you're done. Idle detection keeps your entries honest.

Projects & Clients

Group work by project and tie projects to clients for accurate billing.

Expenses

Log billable and non-billable expenses alongside your tracked time.

Invoices

Generate PDF invoices from your time entries and expenses, in your preferred currency.

Reports

See where your hours go with breakdowns by project, client, and category.
